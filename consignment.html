Consignment

We accept new and used designer clothes, jewelry, shoes and accessories (SEASONAL). All clothing must be cleaned, pressed, stain-free with all buttons and zippers intact, on hangers and in excellent condition.

Three months consignment period, after three months consigner collects a check for items sold and a donation receipt for items donated.

If you are interested in consigning with us or want to inquired more information about our consignment policy, please call ahead and make an appointment in advance.

Round Robin donates all unsold items to organizations that help women in transition
